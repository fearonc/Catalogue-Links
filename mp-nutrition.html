Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between Master/Child and Linked SKUs?
A: Master/Child (Complex SKUs) = A single product displayed on list pages using the master SKU, with the child SKUs creating the drop down options on the PDP. Child SKUs have barcodes and hold stock in the warehouse, Master SKUs have no barcode and are purely for displaying the PDP on-site.

Linked SKUs = 2 or more completely separate SKUs which each appear individually on list pages, but have a “link” to the other SKUs within the linked group. Linked SKUs can be 2 or more Non-Complex SKUs, or 2 or more Master SKUs.
Q: Can I have more than one barcode on a SKU?
A: Yes! We can load multiple barcodes to one THG SKU. The Catalogue and Warehouse systems are designed to work this way.
Q: Why is a Product not displaying on site?
A: This can be a number of reasons:
  • It’s not assigned to site in Catalogue
  • The Availability in TeePee hasn’t been set correctly
  • The product has no stock and the site assignment is on “Show when available”
Q: Why is a product not on a list page?
A: This will depend on how the list page is setup in TeePee. It will be an issue of the SKU not having the correct Attribution that the list page (DDL) is looking for.
Q: How do I update a brand Key?
A: We don’t deal with brand Key’s directly. We require the brand name to load, which will have its own key assigned in the system, so only provide the brand itself, not a key.
Q: How can I search for an existing SKU?
A: You can use Catalogue UI to search for existing SKUs, Title, Barcode, Catalogue numbers.
Don’t use Tabernus to search for products as it is outdated and unreliable.
